Abbalove International Ministries' Youth Summer Camp
Taking place on June 27th - 29th, 2014 in Retreat Agape, Lake Perris, CA
Price of Admission: $80 USD


The Youth Summer Camp is a place where young people can explore and encounter the living God. This three-day impactful weekend is designed to create moments where individuals will make lasting relationships and discover the love of God.


Campers will learn and grow by participating in eye-opening sessions, interactional group discussions, fun activities, and uplifting worship.
